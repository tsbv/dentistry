Tooth Replacement Options Following Extraction

After a tooth extraction in Fairfax, VA, picking the right replacement is key for your oral health. Our fairfax dentist va team offers custom solutions to bring back your smile's look and function. We help you choose options that fit your needs to avoid jawbone loss and keep your face shape.

Dental Implants

Dental implants are titanium posts that blend with your jawbone. They provide a strong base for crowns. The benefits are:

  • They last for decades, making them very durable
  • They help keep your bone healthy by stimulating it
  • They feel and work like your own teeth

Bridges

A dental bridge fills gaps by attaching to nearby teeth. It offers:

  • It's a fixed, non-removable option
  • It improves chewing and speaking
  • It's made to match your tooth color

Partial Dentures

Partial dentures are removable and replace several teeth. They provide:

  • They're comfortable and made with flexible materials
  • They adjust to fit your existing teeth
  • They're easy to clean and care for
