21 Celebrities with Dental Implants, Bridges, and Veneers

In Hollywood, a dazzling smile is more than just an accessory—it’s often the key to stardom. While some celebrities are born with naturally perfect teeth, many owe their enviable grins to cosmetic dentistry. Procedures like dental implants, bridges, and veneers have helped countless stars achieve flawless, camera-ready smiles.

Curious to see which celebrities have enhanced their teeth? Here’s a list of 21 stars who’ve had dental work, along with the stories behind their stunning transformations.

Key Findings

  • Common Procedures:

    Veneers are the most frequently used cosmetic dental treatment among celebrities, followed by dental implants and bridges for more extensive restoration needs.

  • Reasons for Treatment:

    Celebrities seek dental work for various reasons, including accident repair (like Christie Brinkley), correcting misalignment (like Tom Cruise), and addressing wear and tear (like George Clooney).

  • Transformation Impact:

    Dental procedures have helped celebrities enhance their careers by creating camera-ready smiles while maintaining natural-looking results.

  • Treatment Versatility:

    Cosmetic dentistry can address multiple issues simultaneously, from discoloration and gaps to missing teeth and misalignment, as demonstrated by various celebrity cases.

  • 1. Tom Cruise

    Tom Cruise's natural teeth, seen in early films like Risky Business, were far from Hollywood-perfect. With noticeable gaps, chips, and misalignment, the actor's smile underwent a dramatic transformation. By the time he starred in Mission Impossible, Cruise had braces and porcelain veneers that closed gaps and created a brighter, more symmetrical appearance. Today, his flawless smile complements his leading-man charm.

  • 2. George Clooney

    Known for his timeless appeal, George Clooney wasn’t always known for his impeccable smile. Early in his career, Clooney’s teeth were visibly shorter, likely due to bruxism (teeth grinding). This habit, which can wear down teeth over time, may have contributed to his need for cosmetic dentistry. Veneers were used to restore the length and brightness of his teeth, resulting in a polished, classic Hollywood smile.

  • 3. Mike Tyson

    Boxing legend Mike Tyson’s smile has undergone several transformations. Known for his trademark gap-toothed grin, Tyson initially closed the gap with veneers. Later, he had the gap restored as a nod to his original look. Tyson’s boxing career also left him with missing teeth, which were replaced with dental implants. His story showcases how cosmetic dentistry can both restore function and maintain a signature appearance.

  • 4. Miley Cyrus

    As Disney Channel’s Hannah Montana, Miley Cyrus charmed fans with her slightly crooked but adorable smile. As her career progressed, Miley opted for veneers to achieve the straighter, whiter teeth she now flaunts. Her transformation from a Disney starlet to a global pop icon was complemented by her glowing smile.

  • 5. Victoria Beckham

    Before her rise to fame as Posh Spice, Victoria Beckham had less-than-perfect teeth. Her left front tooth was noticeably angled toward the midline. Veneers reshaped and brightened her smile, giving her the sophisticated and polished look she’s now famous for. Beckham’s dental transformation is a testament to the power of cosmetic enhancements.

  • 6. Christie Brinkley

    The supermodel has always been admired for her beauty, but a helicopter crash left her with two broken molars. Dental implants replaced the damaged teeth, seamlessly restoring her smile. Brinkley often praises the natural look and feel of her implants, crediting them for helping her maintain her iconic appearance.

  • 7. Gary Busey

    After a severe motorcycle accident in 1988, Gary Busey required extensive dental restoration. The injuries he sustained to his face necessitated implants and other dental work to repair his smile. While much of the work was functional, it also enhanced his overall appearance.

  • 8. Morgan Freeman

    Morgan Freeman’s captivating voice isn’t his only standout feature. His smile has also received a makeover. Freeman’s earlier look included a gap between his front teeth and some discoloration, both of which were corrected with veneers or bonding. The result is a brighter, more uniform smile that adds to his ageless appeal.

  • 9. Demi Moore

    Demi Moore’s teeth began to show signs of aging over time, including yellowing and a less vibrant appearance. To rejuvenate her look, Moore turned to veneers, which lengthened and brightened her teeth. The actress now sports a youthful, radiant smile that enhances her timeless beauty.

  • 10. Cheryl Cole

    Cheryl Cole’s early career featured pointed canines and slightly misaligned teeth. Veneers helped reshape her smile, giving her an even, red-carpet-ready appearance. The transformation is a perfect example of how subtle changes can make a significant impact.

  • 11. Ben Affleck

    Before his Hollywood breakthrough, Ben Affleck’s teeth were shorter and had visible gaps. As part of his preparation for Armageddon, producer Jerry Bruckheimer arranged for the actor to receive dental caps. These enhancements lengthened his teeth, eliminated gaps, and brightened his smile, contributing to his leading-man allure.

  • 12. Catherine Zeta-Jones

    Catherine Zeta-Jones was teased as a child for her crooked teeth. Braces corrected some alignment issues, but the actress later turned to veneers for a more comprehensive transformation. Today, her brilliant smile is one of her most striking features.

  • 13. Celine Dion

    Before achieving international fame, Celine Dion had a gummy smile and uneven teeth. Her transformation included gum contouring and veneers, which reshaped her teeth and balanced her smile. The result is a dazzling, symmetrical look that perfectly complements her star status.

  • 14. Ed Helms

    Ed Helms’s role in The Hangover required him to look as though he’d lost a tooth. Luckily, Helms had dental implants from years earlier, allowing him to temporarily remove one for the role. This clever use of implants highlighted their durability and natural appearance.

  • 15. Hilary Duff

    Disney Channel star Hilary Duff had a slightly crooked smile early in her career. After chipping a tooth during a performance, she opted for veneers to repair the damage. The transformation gave her a straighter, whiter smile that complements her bubbly persona.

  • 16. Zac Efron

    Zac Efron’s transformation from his gap-toothed days in High School Musical to his leading roles is remarkable. While it’s unclear if he used Invisalign or veneers, his straighter, brighter teeth suggest he benefited from expert cosmetic dentistry.

  • 17. 50 Cent

    After being shot in the face in 2000, rapper 50 Cent was left with a missing tooth and other dental issues. Veneers and implants helped restore his smile, while whitening treatments enhanced the overall look. His dental journey showcases how even significant damage can be repaired with modern techniques.

  • 18. Chris Rock

    Chris Rock’s earlier smile featured upper teeth that jutted out, forming an upside-down “v” shape. After achieving success with films like Lethal Weapon, Rock invested in veneers and implants to create a more even, polished smile.

  • 19. Snooki (Nicole Polizzi)

    Reality TV star Snooki was self-conscious about her crooked teeth during her early Jersey Shore days. She opted for Lumineers, a brand of ultra-thin veneers, to achieve a straighter and whiter smile. The transformation gave her the confidence to show off her grin more often.

  • 20. Jim Carrey

    Jim Carrey’s chipped front tooth, visible in Dumb and Dumber, was a real-life dental injury from his childhood. For the film, he had bonding temporarily removed to create the comedic look. Today, Carrey’s smile benefits from veneers, which enhance both durability and aesthetics.

How to Achieve a Celebrity Smile

Whether you want a subtle enhancement or a dramatic transformation, cosmetic dentistry can help you achieve the smile of your dreams. Procedures like veneers, dental implants, and bridges can address imperfections, improve functionality, and boost your confidence.

Steps to a Hollywood Smile:

  1. Consultation: Meet with a qualified cosmetic dentist to discuss your goals.
  2. Treatment Plan: Choose the procedure that best suits your needs, whether it’s veneers for aesthetics, implants for missing teeth, or bridges for structural support.
  3. Care and Maintenance: Proper oral hygiene and regular dental visits ensure the longevity of your new smile.
